Reading the chart

CW operation is permitted throughout all amateur bands. White stripes show CW-only license privileges; red stripes show a voluntary CW operating sub-band where data remains authorized. E = Amateur Extra, A = Advanced, G = General, and T = Technician.

Power & source

Use the minimum transmitter power necessary. Unless noted, no station may exceed 1.5 kW PEP. Data is based on the ARRL U.S. Amateur Radio Bands chart, revision 1/16/2026.

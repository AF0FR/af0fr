Reading the chart

CW operation is permitted throughout all amateur bands. Segment colors show additional mode privileges. E = Amateur Extra, A = Advanced, G = General, T = Technician, and N = Novice.

Power & source

Use the minimum transmitter power necessary. Unless noted, no station may exceed 1.5 kW PEP. Data is based on the ARRL U.S. Amateur Radio Bands chart, revision 1/16/2026.
